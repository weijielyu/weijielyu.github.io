University of California, Merced

University of California, Merced became an Interfolio client partner in 2016. University of California, Merced, a research-oriented university, has adopted Interfolio Review, Promotion & Tenure for its faculty members, focusing on the workflow capabilities for promotion opportunities. The technology is simple, easy to use, and lives in the Cloud for 24/7 access. It also features a modern interface that enables both staff and faculty to engage with the software. 

See what Mubeena Salaam, Business Systems Analyst at University of California, Merced, has to say about why they chose Interfolio Review, Promotion, and Tenure.
